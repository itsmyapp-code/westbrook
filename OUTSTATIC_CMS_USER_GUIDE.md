# 🌟 The Simple Guide to Updating Your Website

This guide explains in **simple English** how to edit text, prices, and photos on your website using your Outstatic dashboard. You do **not** need any coding knowledge to use this!

---

## 🚀 How to Log In & Save
1. Go to your dashboard URL: **`https://westbrook.vercel.app/outstatic`**
2. Click **Log in with GitHub**.
3. In the left menu, click any section (like **Prices** or **Heroslider**).
4. Make your changes and click the black **Save** button (top right).

> **The 30-Second Rule:**
> When you click **Save**, your website updates itself automatically in the background. It takes about **30 seconds** for the live website to finish updating. After 30 seconds, refresh your web page (`Ctrl + F5`) and your changes will be live!

---

## 🖼️ How to Change ANY Photo (3 Simple Steps)
No matter what you are editing (a slide, a service, or a page), changing a picture always works the exact same way:

1. Look at the **Right Sidebar** on your screen.
2. Scroll down to **Cover Image** *(if it is closed, click the little arrow `^` next to the words Cover Image)*.
3. Click the **`From file`** button, choose a photo from your computer, and click **Save**!

---

## 🗺️ Where to Click to Change Each Part of Your Website

Here is a simple cheat sheet telling you exactly which left-menu button to click based on what you want to change on the website:

### 1. The Large Top Banner Photos on the Homepage (`/`)
* **Click on:** `Heroslider`
* **What you see on the website:** The 3 big rotating photos right under the top menu bar.
* **How to change it:**
  * Click into **`slide-1`**, **`slide-2`**, or **`slide-3`**.
  * **To change the headline:** Type inside the **`Title`** box.
  * **To change the photo:** Use the **`Cover Image`** box on the right sidebar.

---

### 2. The 3 Main Service Cards on the Homepage (`/`)
* **Click on:** `Services`
* **What you see on the website:** The 3 cards with blue icons (`Commercial`, `Dry Cleaning`, `Launderette`) shown on the Homepage.
* **How to change it:**
  * Click into the card you want to edit.
  * **To change the title:** Type inside the **`Title`** box.
  * **To change what page it links to:** Look at **`serviceCategory`** at the bottom of your screen and type either `commercial`, `dry-cleaning`, or `launderette`.

---

### 3. The 4-Step "How It Works" Section on the Homepage (`/`)
* **Click on:** `Workflows`
* **What you see on the website:** The numbered steps (`Step 1` through `Step 4`) explaining your laundry process.
* **How to change it:**
  * Click into **`step-1`**, **`step-2`**, **`step-3`**, or **`step-4`**.
  * **To change the step title:** Type inside the **`Title`** box.
  * **To change the order:** Type `1`, `2`, `3`, or `4` inside the **`stepNumber`** box.

---

### 4. Customer Reviews & Testimonials (`/`)
* **Click on:** `Testimonials`
* **What you see on the website:** The sliding customer reviews shown near the bottom of the Homepage.
* **How to change it:**
  * Click into any review (`rev-1`, `rev-2`, `rev-3`).
  * **To change the review quote:** Type the customer's exact words directly into the large **Main Center Text Box**.
  * **To change the customer's name:** Look at the right sidebar under **`Author`** and type their name inside the **`Name`** box.
  * **To change the star rating (1 to 5):** Type `5` inside the **`rating`** box at the bottom of your screen.

---

### 5. Prices & Garment Tariffs across Subpages
* **Click on:** `Prices`
* **What you see on the website:** The price cards and tables shown on **`/launderette`**, **`/dry-cleaning`**, and **`/commercial`**.
* **How to change it:**
  * Click into any price item (`suit-two-piece`, `laun-18kg-wash`, etc.).
  * **To change the item name:** Type inside the **`Title`** box (`e.g., 2-Piece Suit`).
  * **To change the price:** Type the price inside the **`priceValue`** box (`e.g., £16.50`).
  * **To choose which page displays this price:** Look inside the **`serviceCategory`** box at the bottom and type:
    * `launderette` *(if you want it on the Launderette page)*
    * `dry-cleaning` *(if you want it on the Dry Cleaning page)*
    * `commercial` *(if you want it on the Commercial page)*

---

### 6. Subpage Header Banners & Paragraph Copy
* **Click on:** `Pages`
* **What you see on the website:** The main header titles and paragraphs across **About (`/about`)**, **Commercial (`/commercial`)**, **Dry Cleaning (`/dry-cleaning`)**, **Launderette (`/launderette`)**, and **Contact (`/contact`)**.
* **How to change it:**
  * Click into `about`, `commercial`, `dry-cleaning`, `launderette`, or `contact`.
  * **To change the top header title:** Type inside the **`Title`** box.
  * **To change the page text:** Type your headings, bullet points, and paragraphs directly into the large **Main Center Text Box**.
